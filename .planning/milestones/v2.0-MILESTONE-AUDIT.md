### Warnings (non-blocking)

1. **NEXT_PUBLIC_API_BASE_URL**: `WaitlistForm.tsx` relies on the env var being *absent* at build time (not explicitly `""`). The `.env*` glob in `landing/.dockerignore` prevents `.env.local` from entering the build context. Risk: operator sets the var in CI or a `.env` file not covered by the glob. No breakage today; document in deployment runbook.

2. **Stale REQUIREMENTS.md traceability**: LAND-01..04, UI-03/04/05, and INFRA-06 traceability entries are not updated post-execution. No runtime impact.
